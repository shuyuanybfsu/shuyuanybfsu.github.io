About Astrill VPN

2009 birth of Astrill: Astrill was founded back in 2009 as a response to increasing demand for personal VPN services by home users and travelers. We are a registered Seychelles company focused mostly towards travelers and expats, who use Astrill VPN service for encrypted and unrestricted Internet access at any time and place.

20+ team members: Our small team of cryptographers and programmers grew quickly as more experts and customer support agents were joining us. After the first year of being in business, Astrill expanded to USA and EU markets. Today, Astrill is the leader in the global personal VPN market, helping millions to stay safe online.

360+ servers: Currently, we offer hundreds of VPN servers in 50+ countries with a diversity of available VPN technologies. Our well-trained customer support staff provides 24/7 customer support service via e-mail, live chat and phone.

10+ platforms: Astrill VPN service works on personal computers (Windows, Mac and Linux), handheld devices (iOS, Android, Blackberry) as well as compatible WiFi routers (DD-WRT, Tomato, ASUS Merlin). We are constantly expanding our network and improving our plug-and-play software solutions.
